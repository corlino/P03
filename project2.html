Urban Alleyway

Spring 2023

This project is a 3D modelled an urban alleyway made completely using Maya. This project used a variety of assets, texturing, and lighting techniques which combined to make a visually interesting composition.


The lighting is represented by the red wireframes which were pointed through the window frames to display shadows on the ground in the shape of the window.


These are shots from the final render which shows the whole scene from two different angles.

Reflection

This project resulted in a clean scene that had a visually interested aesthetic and showed potential narrative. This was a good early experiment that combined my interests in urban artwork and 3D modelling.
