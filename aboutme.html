Who Am I?

My name is Colin and I'm a student studying graphic and UI/UX design at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University. In my two years of studies, I have developed skills relating to user interface and user interactivity from various courses within my program. I am also experienced in sound design, videography, animation and marketing.

Where Am I?

I am primarily located in Vancouver, British Columbia, where I am currently finishing my studies. I also am occasionally in the Greater New York City Area visiting friends and family.

Employment Interests

I'm an energetic individual seeking work in a creative environment where I can offer my graphic editing and UI/UX design skills. To compliment these skills, I also have significant marketing and business administration knowledge. In my experience I work best in collaborative teams and have been comfortable in fast paced situations where time management was important for success.
