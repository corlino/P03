Beam Buddy: Flashlight Product Development

Fall 2023

In this project, we were tasked with creating a flashlight for a demographic of 6 year old children. my partner and I developed the Beam Buddy based on an initial prototype built from styrofoam. The inital concept was based on a camera that a toddler would easily be able to hold, point, and shoot.
