Look Up At The Stars

Spring 2023

This project was for a client who requested a galaxy type of mural on the ceiling of their theatre room of a new house that they were developing and moving into.


This was made using Loop spraypaint and cardboard stencils.






Happy Space

Fall 2022

This project was for a friend who requested a painting for their office cubible art gallery. This painting compliments the works of two other coworkers who also have their place on her office wall.


This was made using acrylic paint and brushes.






The Cans

Fall 2022

This was a fun personal project of mine to contribute to Burnaby's Leeside Skatepark graffiti wall. This was made around Halloween time and I wanted the message to be relatable to a lot of other artists that paint in this tunnel, acknowledging that the most relaxing time to paint is at night. This managed to stay up in the tunnel for around 3 months before being covered by another artist.


This was made using Rust-oleum spraypaint.






Fingers

Spring 2023

This was a quick project I had planned to get a better grasp on creating hands and the shapes required to do so. I explored different colours and gradients that overall made the piece pop and be seen from far away. This has since been covered by other artists.


This was made using Rust-oleum spraypaint.
